About

Inspired by
nature's logic

Welcome! I'm a high school student fascinated by the intelligence embedded in living systems. I'm interested in biomimicry, sustainable design, and soft robotics — asking what 3.8 billion years of evolution can teach us about building a better world.

I believe the most elegant solutions already exist in nature; we just need to spend the time to find them.
